Laptecdesk

Expand your Workplace possibilities with the new LapTecDesk Designed to be your optimal desk for long working hours in any surroundings. The Laptecdesk is the optimal Lapdesk-Solution for your, doesn’t matter if you are a pupil, student or professional, wanting to use it for school, work or in your free time. Every part is carefully considered, giving you a premium, handmade product. All parts, except for Scharniere, are made out of Aluminum, which gives the stability and light weight. The Scharniere are made out of stainless steel, to give it its needed endurance and longgevity. The top is made out of massive wood, and painted in house with Egg-Tempera Paint. Egg Tempera is a very old Paint Technique, used through history for furniture and Art Paintings. The paint is characterized by is vibrant Paint character.
